The journey of AL-AMREEN started with the launch of 18k gold watches studded with precious stones in 1994. But, it soon grew into a 22K jeweller who presented a stunning range of gold and diamond jewellery. The term AL-AMREEN was coined by Mr. MODH. SAQIB ALAM by marrying the words ‘MODH’ meaning body and ‘SAQIB’ meaning a gold ornament and ALAM’s very first state-of-art jewellery factory with a proper karigaar park was set up in Hosur in Kanpur.
